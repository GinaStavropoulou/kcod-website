On October 28th, Stefano Calzati took part as an invited speaker to the workshop of [The New Open](https://www.newopen.design/), the flagship project of the Faculty of Architecture and Built Environment at TU Delft. Together with international architects, designers, and scholars, Calzati discussed the possibility of transiting "From Open Data to Data Commons: Enacting a Data Ecosystem for the Built Environment."
