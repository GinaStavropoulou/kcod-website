The Call for Paper for the [AGILE 2023 Conference](https://agile-online.org/conference-2023/call-for-papers-2023) is out! In 2023 the annual conference of the Association of Geographic Information Laboratories in Europe (AGILE) will be held in Delft, between 13th and 16th of June. This year’s topic is "spatial data for design" and explores the fertile and varied connections between geoinformation and a whole range of design activities. **Deadline: 22nd of December!**
