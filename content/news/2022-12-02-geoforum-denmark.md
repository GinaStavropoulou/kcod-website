On November 18th, Prof. Bastiaan van Loenen delivered a keynote lecture at the [Danish National GIS conference](https://kortdage.dk/). The keynote, titled "Towards Geographical Information Ecosystems", attracted more than 800 people, among academic scholars, GIS practitioners, actors in the public and private sector, civil servants, non-institutional actors, and it was the occasion to disseminate ODECO project and advance an ecosystemic understanding for geo information.
