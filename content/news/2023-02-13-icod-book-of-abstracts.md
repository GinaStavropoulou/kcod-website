The [Book of Abstracts](https://todo-project.eu/sites/default/files/inline-files/ICOD%202022%20Book%20of%20abstracts.pdf) of the 2022 International Conference on Open Data, held between November 28th and December 2nd at the University of Zagreb, is out!

Among others, the book contains interventions by various members of KCOP, as well as geomatics students of TU Delft.
