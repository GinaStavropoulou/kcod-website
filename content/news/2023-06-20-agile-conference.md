The [2023 Conference of the Association of Geographic Information Laboratories in Europe (AGILE)](https://agile-online.org/conference-2023/) was held at TU Delft from the 13th to the 16th of June. It hosted a diverse cohort of practitioners, scholars and PhD students around the theme of “Spatial Data for Design”.
