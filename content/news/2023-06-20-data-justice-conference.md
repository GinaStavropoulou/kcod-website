Stefano Calzati attended (online) the 2023 Data Justice Conference: Exploring Social Justice in the Age of Datafication” organised by the Data Justice Lab at Cardiff University. He presented the paper “Conceptualising the Data Republic: Fair Data Ecosystems as a Way to Balance Individual and Collective Data Rights”.
